## 📌 Project Overview

A **Cohort Retention Analysis** that groups customers by their acquisition month and tracks engagement over time. This time-based behavioral analysis reveals how well a business retains customers, identifies the most critical drop-off periods, and highlights which acquisition cohorts generate the highest long-term value.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- 📊 What percentage of customers return each month after first purchase?
- 📉 When is the biggest customer drop-off point?
- 🏆 Which acquisition cohorts have the best long-term retention?
- 💰 How does revenue evolve across cohorts over time?
- 📅 What is the optimal window for retention interventions?
